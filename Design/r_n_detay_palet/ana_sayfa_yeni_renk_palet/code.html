Our Monograph

Palet was founded on a singular obsession: the tactile honesty of suede. We believe footwear should feel as intimate as a second skin.

Every pair starts in our Tuscan atelier, where hides are selected for their 'nap' and resilience. It's a slow process, one that defies the rapid pace of modern fashion to favor lasting character.
